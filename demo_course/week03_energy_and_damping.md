# Week 3 Notes: Energy Loss and Damping

Mechanical energy shifts between gravitational potential energy and kinetic energy during each swing. Air drag and pivot friction remove part of that energy, so peak amplitude shrinks over time even when period changes only slightly.

Underdamped motion still oscillates while envelope decays. Critically damped motion returns to equilibrium quickly without oscillating, and overdamped motion returns more slowly than critical case.

When graph looks strange, first compare trial setup notes against data table. Sudden damping jump often comes from changed friction source, not hidden force or broken model.
